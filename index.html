About us

A little bit of our story.

  • 2015-2018

    How we started

    It all started in our kitchen at home, when we decided to make cakes for our family and friends for different occasions. For about three years, the magic was happening there, thanks to the help of my mother and my aunt.

  • May 2018

    The bakery was a reality

    We finally had the opportunity to open a small bakery in the city center together. We had more and more clients every day.

  • Nowadays

    Irina's Bakery in five more cities

    Now we have bakeries in five more cities. Our staff has increased and we are about to expand in three more cities, where we will need even more sunny and experienced people in our big team.
